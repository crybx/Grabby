About Grabby

Grabby makes reading online stories easier.

Features

  • One-click chapter saving from any web page
  • Support for 50+ websites with custom cleanup logic
  • Bulk grabbing for sequential chapter saving
  • Story tracking to manage your reading progress
  • Clean, readable HTML output without ads or clutter
  • Keyboard shortcut support (Alt+G)

How to Use

Click the clipboard icon that appears at the top-right corner of any web page to grab its content:

The extracted chapter will be downloaded as an HTML file with the format:

{chapter#_and_story_title}_{domain}.html

Story Tracker

Track your reading progress and manage your story collection.
